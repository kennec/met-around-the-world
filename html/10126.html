Korea Foundation Internship at The Metropolitan Museum of Art

South Korea

2011

This internship offers practical museum training to young Koreans interested in pursuing a career in the fields of arts and museums. The participant is hosted by the Department of Asian Art and engages in independent research related to the Museum’s collection. The participant is also exposed to a range of activities across the Museum.

Made possible by the Korea Foundation.
