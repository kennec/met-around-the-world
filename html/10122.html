The Victoria and Albert Museum / The Metropolitan Museum of Art Staff Exchange Program enables curators from each institution to conduct art-historical research projects and share ideas and working methods with their colleagues. One participant studied the Met's European sculpture collection and galleries as background for the organization of the V&A's new Medieval and Renaissance galleries.
