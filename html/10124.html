American Academy in Rome Fellowship

Italy

2000–present

Each year, the American Academy in Rome offers a fellowship to a Metropolitan Museum curator, conservator, or scientist, which includes residency at the Academy. The participant becomes part of the Academy’s community of artists and scholars, whose subjects comprise the humanities and fine arts. A recent participant conducted research on French artists active in Italy between 1780 and 1850, with a particular emphasis on painters affiliated with the French Academy in Rome.

Made possible by the Merksamer Travel Fund.
