The State Hermitage Museum / The Metropolitan Museum of Art Staff Exchange Program

Russia

1993–2000/2003–present

Since its inception in the early 1990s, The State Hermitage Museum / The Metropolitan Museum of Art Staff Exchange Program has supported more than sixty participants from both institutions. The opportunity to share research, knowledge, and expertise is ongoing. Projects have ranged from consultations among conservators about a specific work of art, later influencing the final treatment of that work in exciting new ways, to the study of Catherine the Great's collection of furniture by the workshop of Abraham and David Roentgen.

Made possible by Trust for Mutual Understanding.
