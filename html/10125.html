Since its inception a decade ago, the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation Cultural Exchange Program has offered Chinese professionals the opportunity to study the structure and administration of American museums and how they organize cultural and educational activities. The first participant spent nine months in the United States, part of that time in residence at the Met and part traveling nationally to other museums and institutions. He familiarized himself with best practices and, once back home, translated these ideas to museums in Beijing as well as to regional museums throughout China.
